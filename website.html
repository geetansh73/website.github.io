Confused about which subject is to be taken in B.Tech

Well, there can be many aspects to be considered while choosing a subject (stream) for B.Tech. No branch is less or more important, each of them has it's own importance, it solely depends on you, in which stream you want to make your career. See there are enough jobs for every stream, what a company need is quality engineers, SO if you are dead serious for your better future and giving parallel dedication towards it, you will definitely going to succeed.

But then there are some factors that you should consider before making any decision, like-

Area of your interest:

Before being confused with other's opinions, ask yourself, in which stream you have more interest, what attracts you more, by this approach you'll find a better way to choose a branch.

Approach:

If any family member or relative is already an engineer or can help you in the future to get a job then consult them, if they can provide you reference when you pass out. Look we all know that in INDIA nothing is required more than a source (reference) to get a job. So if any reference you have then pursue B.Tech in that particular stream.

College Profile:

In every college (even in IITs, NITs) some specific branches are more valuable than others, in terms of Placements, Faculties, Laboratory, so if you've decided a college then start gathering information about these facts before choosing/applying for any stream.
